Our Story

Clothing that doesn't pick a side.

Néu started with a simple frustration: most fashion is designed for one body, one gender, one way of moving through the world. We build the opposite. Every piece in this store is cut, tested and priced to work on anyone who wants to wear it.

What we believe

Three ideas we don't compromise on

Unisex by design, not as an afterthought

We don't take a men's pattern and relabel it. Every silhouette is built from the ground up to fit a range of bodies well, from the shoulder seam to the hem.

Fewer, better pieces

We would rather sell you one jacket you wear for five years than five you replace every season. That shapes everything from our fabric choices to our pricing.

Honest pricing, no fake discounts

When something is on sale, it's genuinely reduced. We don't inflate prices just to mark them down again later.

Based in Johannesburg. Shipped everywhere in South Africa.
