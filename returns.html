Are returns free?
Yes, full-price items are returned free of charge. Sale items can be returned for store credit only, unless faulty.
Can I exchange for a different size instead?
Yes. Select "Exchange for different size" above and mention your preferred size in the notes field. We'll ship the replacement as soon as your original item is scanned by the courier, no need to wait for it to arrive back with us first.
What if my item arrives faulty or wrong?
Submit the form above with as much detail as you can. We'll arrange a free collection and send a replacement or full refund, your choice.
How long does a refund take to reflect?
Once processed on our end, refunds typically reflect within 5 to 7 working days, depending on your bank.
